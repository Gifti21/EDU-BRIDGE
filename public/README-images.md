# Image Setup Instructions

## School Campus Background Image

To use your beautiful school campus image as the background:

1. **Save your school campus image** as `school-campus.jpg` in the `public` folder
2. **Make sure the image is optimized** for web use (recommended size: 1920x1080 or similar)
3. **The image will automatically appear** as the background in:
   - Hero section (main landing area)
   - Campus showcase section (middle of the page)
   - Call-to-action section (bottom area)

## Image Optimization Tips

- **File size**: Keep under 500KB for fast loading
- **Format**: JPG for photos, PNG for graphics with transparency
- **Dimensions**: 1920x1080 or 1600x900 work well for backgrounds
- **Quality**: 80-85% compression is usually optimal
